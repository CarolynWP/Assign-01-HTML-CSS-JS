Below is a list of terminology/vocabulary in Wings Of Fire:

  • Scavenger/Reading Monkey: Humans
  • Dragonets: Young/teenage dragons
  • Wing: A large group of dragons
  • Winglet: A smaller group of dragons
  • Scales: Pantalan currency
  • Animus: A dragon with the power to enchant objects/beings to perform commands
  • Firescales: A dragon who was born with too much fire. They burn anything they touch
  • Tribes: Dragon species
